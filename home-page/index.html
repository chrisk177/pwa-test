Bringing together female entrepreneurs across the globe to empower one another.

Striving for gender equality, LinkedBosses is here to build a community that empowers women. Female entrepreneurs are at a disadvantage in the workplace- from receiving less venture capital funding compared to men to having to do extra work to prove their worth. LinkedBosses is here to connect you with others to bounce ideas, learn tools, meet with investors, and share your business!
